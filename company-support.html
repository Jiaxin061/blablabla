❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I interpret my system health score?
Your system health score is calculated based on several factors including CPU performance, memory usage, disk health, and network connectivity. A score above 80 indicates your system is in good condition, while scores below 60 may require attention to specific areas highlighted in your reports.
What should I do when I receive a security alert?
When you receive a security alert, first check the details of the alert in the Security section of your Reports page. Follow the recommended actions provided with the alert. For critical alerts, contact our support team immediately for assistance in resolving the security issue.
How often is my system scanned?
By default, DblaFence performs a basic system scan once per day and a comprehensive scan once per week. You can adjust these settings or run manual scans at any time from your Settings page under the Security section.
Can I export my system reports?
Yes, you can export your system reports in various formats including PDF, Excel, and CSV. Navigate to the Reports page and use the export options at the top of the page. You can also schedule regular exports in your notification settings.
How do I update my DblaFence software?
DblaFence updates automatically by default. You can check your current version and update status in the Settings page under the System section. If you've disabled automatic updates, you'll receive notifications when new updates are available.
